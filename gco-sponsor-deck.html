Our Story

Built for
Creators

What started in 2025 as the Golf Creator Open was built in just 90 days — driven by a simple idea: create a space where golf content creators of all backgrounds could come together, compete, and connect through the game and the content that unites them.

The response was immediate. The event proved there was a real demand for something different in golf — something more inclusive, more modern, and built for creators. In 2026, we evolved. The Golf Creator Tour was created to open the door wider, giving creators the opportunity to earn their place through a year-long points system.

The path to the championship is no longer selected — it's earned.
