How to Earn Points

Three Ways to Climb the Leaderboard

Every point you earn moves you closer to the Golf Creator Open.

01
⛳
Point Source 1
Creator Matches
Challenge other golf creators to head-to-head matches — match play, stroke play, skins, 2v2 scramble, and more. Win matches, earn points. Compete from any course, anywhere in the world.
02
🎯
Point Source 2
Sponsored Challenges
Brands launch challenges inside the GCT app. Complete the challenge, post your content, and earn points. From iron masterclasses to longest drive competitions — every challenge is an opportunity to score.
03
📈
Point Source 3
Social Media Growth
Grow your audience and earn points for it. GCT rewards creators who build their following — the bigger your reach, the more you contribute to the network and the higher you climb the board.
