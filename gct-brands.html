The Core Opportunity

Sponsored Challenges

Sponsored Challenges are one of the three ways GCT creators earn points toward the Golf Creator Open. When you launch a challenge, 100 creators are motivated to complete it — not because they have to, but because their ranking depends on it. That's built-in creator incentive no traditional influencer deal can match.

Launch a Challenge
✓
Your brief becomes their mission. Creators earn points for completing your challenge — they're competing to create your content, not just posting for a paycheck.
✓
Authentic by design. Every challenge is tied to a real competitive context — iron technique, driving distance, putting pressure. The content is genuine because the competition is real.
✓
Point value = creator motivation. You set the challenge tier — Elite, Premium, or Standard — which determines the point value and the level of creator participation and urgency.
✓
Scale from day one. Tap into 100 pre-vetted golf content creators immediately. No sourcing, no cold outreach, no one-off negotiations. One campaign brief, 100 creator reach.

The Partnership Journey

Launch. Reach. Capture. Grow.

From your first conversation to your challenge going live — a clear, measurable path to results.

01
🤝
Step 1
Partner
Apply as a GCT brand partner. Work with our team to design your Sponsored Challenge — choose the challenge type, content format, point tier (Elite, Premium, or Standard), and campaign duration. Onboarding takes days, not months.
02
🎯
Step 2
Launch
Your Sponsored Challenge goes live inside the GCT app. 100 creators see your brief, understand the point reward, and compete to complete it. The higher the point value, the more urgency — creators fighting for the Open don't miss big challenges.
03
🎬
Step 3
Capture
Creators deliver authentic, competition-driven content tied to your brand. Every post, Reel, and video is created because the creator genuinely competed in your challenge — not because they were paid to pretend. The authenticity is built into the format.
04
📈
Step 4
Grow
Track results through GCT's analytics dashboard. Measure content reach, audience engagement, and challenge participation across every creator. Iterate and scale what works — with a dedicated partner success team helping you every step of the way.
