How to Earn Points

Three Ways to Climb the Board

Points flow from three sources. Stack all three and rise fast. The Top 100 earn the Open — every point matters.

⛳
Source 1
Creator Matches
Challenge other creators to head-to-head matches — Match Play, Stroke Play, Skins, 2v2 Scramble, and more. Accept a challenge, schedule your tee time, play your round, and submit. Win matches, earn points. Compete from any course, anywhere in the world.
🎯
Source 2
Sponsored Challenges
Brands launch challenges directly inside the GCT app — from iron masterclasses to longest drive competitions to lifestyle content. Browse the active challenges, pick your spots, complete the brief, post your content, and earn points. The bigger the challenge, the bigger the point reward.
📈
Source 3
Social Media Growth
GCT rewards creators who build their audience. Connect your channels and earn points as your following grows. The bigger your reach, the more you contribute to the GCT network — and the more points you accumulate toward the Open. Create great content. Grow. Earn.
